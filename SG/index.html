Sonal Giani

Known for her pioneering work in highlighting Lesbian and Bisexual women’s issues as well as LGBTQ youth work, Sonal Giani is a very vocal LGBTQ activist and an actress. She had also co-founded one of India’s largest LGBTQ youth initiatives Yaariyan and Umang, a lesbian-bisexual-transgender initiative in Mumbai. Sonal has been part of film projects, theatre productions and represented the Indian LGBTQ community globally around the time she was working as the Advocacy Manager at the Humsafar Trust. She is best known for featuring in the documentary style television series Connected Hum Tum that surfaced in 2013, where she had shared her real-life experiences and struggles as an Indian bisexual woman.
